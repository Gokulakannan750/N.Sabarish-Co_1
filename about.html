Our Story

From civil works to a trusted regional firm

M/s. N. Sakthivel & Co. is a most disciplined, quality-conscious firm that has attained quality consistently since its inception in 1996, founded by Mr. N. Sakthivel.

Founder Mr. N. Sakthivel began executing civil works in his own name after gaining experience at U.R. Thangamuthu & Co. — itself founded by U.R. Thangamuthu, a retired partner of URC Constructions. That lineage of subject knowledge and discipline still guides how we build.

Today we have proven our skill across private medical colleges, government colleges and schools, TN Police housing and armed-reserve complexes, the Public Works Department, sugar mills and major industrial facilities.

Our mission, vision & values

The principles that guide every decision, every estimate and every nail we drive.

Our Mission

N. Sakthivel & Co. has been established to fulfil our clients’ dream by providing construction services and to nurture strong, long-lasting client relationships. We pledge to be fair and true to our clients and employees, to meet their expectations and earn their trust, and to keep working in an ethical and efficient manner.

Our Vision

To be known for quality, reliability, timely completion and safe working environments — maximising stakeholder value through the continuous adoption of the latest technologies, management practices and innovation.

Our Values

Quality without compromise. Reliability in every commitment. Timely completion of every project. Safe working environments and responsibility towards the community we serve.
