Who We Are

Quality, reliability and timely completion since 2002

Over the years, N. Sakthivel & Co. has made its presence felt in the construction industry by delivering good-quality work, meeting client requirements as committed and completing projects within schedule. The firm has grown from a modest turnover of ₹40 lakhs in 1997 to nearly ₹35 crore in 2024.
